ℹ Reference & Notes click to expand
Fu Rules
  • Base: 20 fu (open/tsumo) · 30 fu (closed ron)
  • Tsumo: +2 fu (skip for Pinfu)
  • Menzen ron: +10 fu
  • Open sequence: 0 · Closed sequence: 0
  • Open triplet simples: 2 · terminals/honors: 4
  • Closed triplet simples: 4 · terminals/honors: 8
  • Open quad simples: 8 · terminals/honors: 16
  • Closed quad simples: 16 · terminals/honors: 32
  • Dragon pair: 2 · single yakuhai wind: 2 · double yakuhai: 4
  • Kanchan / Penchan / Tanki wait: +2 fu
  • Round up to nearest 10 fu
Special Overrides
  • Chiitoitsu: fixed 25 fu, no rounding
  • Pinfu tsumo: fixed 20 fu
  • Kokushi: no fu — tick in yaku list
  • Dora does not affect fu
Scoring Limits
  • Mangan: 5+ han, or basic pts ≥ 2000
  • Haneman: 6–7 han (×1.5)
  • Baiman: 8–10 han (×2)
  • Sanbaiman: 11–12 han (×3)
  • Yakuman: 13+ han (×4)
Notation Legend
  • m = Man / Characters (1m–9m)
  • p = Pin / Circles (1p–9p)
  • s = Sou / Bamboo (1s–9s)
  • z = Honors (1z–7z)
  • 1z East · 2z South · 3z West · 4z North
  • 5z Haku (白) · 6z Hatsu (発) · 7z Chun (中)
  • Example: 123m456p789s 1z1z
Honba

Each honba: +100 pts per payer (tsumo) or +300 pts total (ron).
