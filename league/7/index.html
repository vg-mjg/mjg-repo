Schedule

League will run for about 7 weeks, starting on 23/05 and ending in July. The duration of the league will depend on how many players we have, and the final schedule will be posted later down the line. There are going to be 3 phases: regular season with all the teams, semifinals with the top 8 teams, and a finals with only the top 4 teams from semifinals to settle the winner. Scores will be halved at the start of each stage


A more precise schedule will be posted later on.
