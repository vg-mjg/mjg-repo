Guards of Sekhmet No. 10

In a kingdom from a distant land, a message arrived in the hands of a bored Goddess Sekhmet informing her of a grand mahjong tournament being held in the lands of the distant /mjg/.
Goddess Sekhmet felt a fire burn in her soul as her passion for combat and strife was fanned and flamed. Wether the fight was physical or by throwing tiles at the table did not matter to her. She had to participate in this tournament no matter the cost.
And thus Goddess Sekhmet decided to gather the Queen of her land Nyakopatra and her finest and greatest guardians and set sail.
Her heart set on conquest and glory for her land, she travelled across the deserts and the seas. The journey was perilous and at times the traveling crew did lose their sense of direction, but with the guidance of their goddess and queen they managed to safely arrive at the venue for the tournament. And not a moment too late for they almost missed the deadline!
Soon the Goddess, her queen and the guards will enter a battle of passion, wits, strategy and luck to see who will claim victory and this Goddess has no intent to lose.
What will await the crew in the next few weeks is a tale for another time....
