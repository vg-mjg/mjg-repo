All Characters Owned

Here are the results for all characters owned.

The order in the chart above is in the same order as the survey form, where it's all female characters then male characters, in their release order.
This gives a nice visualisation of how many people actually roll on Bamboo, with the sharp dropoff from Mila (newest girl with 20) to Natsuki (oldest guy with only 6 people owning him).

The chart also shows the popularity of collabs. Older collabs like the Kakegurui and Saki collabs show low numbers for character ownership, while recent ones like the Fate Heavens Feel collab have much higher ownership than characters released around it (a lot less people have rolled Mario and Luigi despite being released around the same time for example).
A notable character in this chart is Yuzu (35), who stands out compared to regular characters released before and after her.
It's no surprise that nobody on /mjg/ cares about Fei Ming, only 1 anon who answered the survey has ever rolled him.
