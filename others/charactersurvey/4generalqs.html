When did anons find /mjg/?

A surprisingly even distribution across the years, /mjg/ has grown steadily it seems. According to the results, around 10% of players in the general found /mjg/ in 2019 when Majsoul first came to EN.
It makes me wonder how the results would change if this question was asked again during league time.

Collab reruns

The next question asked "If you could pick one collab to rerun, which collab would you pick?". The question featured every collab in the past to see what most people are interested in.

Saki is by far the most requested rerun at 36 responses, while Akagi is second at 24. Saki already had a rerun in the past adding four more characters, so it's possible.
Maybe people just want to roll Koromo.

It would be nice for a Hat rerun or for imouto to get released (fucking never ;_;)

How people found out about Riichi Mahjong

The chart shows that most people found out about Mahjong through anime, whether it's Akagi, Saki or something else.
The other answers are quite varied. I'm quite surprised very few learned Riichi through their family, as in my head I thought this common in Asian families. Maybe people learned a different kind of Mahjong this way?
Quite cool that a fifth of the people who answered found out Mahjong through 4chan, whether it's through /mjg/ or some thread on /a/, /v/ or /jp/.
