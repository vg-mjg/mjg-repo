Ranks

The survey asked participants to record their current and highest ranks in both standard 4-player and Sanma 3p.

For the data in this section some anons managed to enter their current rank as higher than their highest ever rank - in these cases I have given them the benefit of doubt and bumped up their highest rank to match.
This was a required question in the survey when it shouldn't've been, so some anons have spoilt their answers, which is why I have removed a few severe outliers (Highest rank Celestial, current rank Novice 2 for example).

4-Player Current vs Highest rank

The chart shows the distribution of ranks in 4-player and the rank compared with their highest. Points on the y = x line means that their current rank is the same than their highest.

4-player vs 3-player ranks

Using both 4-player and 3-player ranking data we can try to find if there's any correlation between being a high rank in yonma vs sanma. For this I used the raw data that users inputted for their current rank, here is the resulting chart.
For a point on this scatter, the higher rank in yonma a point is, the more red it gets. On the other hand, the higher rank in sanma a point is, the more blue it gets.

It clearly shows that the majority of /mjg/ have a higher rank in yonma vs sanma, and shows very little correlation between the ranks. This is further evidence that /mjg/ doesn't give a shit about ranked sanma much at all.
