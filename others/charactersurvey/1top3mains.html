Characters picked in top 3 mains

This next chart shows the total count of characters that are mentioned in peoples top 3 mains. Characters are only counted once - some anons put a single character as all three of their top 3 mains (yuzu and cats).
For the data in this chart I have only counted them once. This should give us a general idea of what characters are popular in /mjg/ and who people like to play.
I left in the "I don't have 3 characters" option for this graph too.

This is a decent distribution of characters, with only 21 out of the 116 character cast not being in anyones top 3 characters as their mains.
All of the unpicked characters are limited or males, meaning every permanent female character was picked by at least someone in their top 3 mains.
